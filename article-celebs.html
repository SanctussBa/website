As you step into this glittering age of golden wisdom, it turns out you're in fantastic company. That's right, even the stars in Hollywood and beyond are lining up to welcome you to the club. They've been where you are, and they've got some pearls of wisdom, a few chuckles, and maybe even a wrinkle or two of advice just for you. So, grab your reading glasses (we know you have them), sit back, and let's hear what these familiar faces have to say about hitting the big five-oh!

"The face you have at age twenty-five is the face God gave you, but the face you have after fifty is the face you earned"

"The man who views the world at fifty the same as he did at twenty has wasted thirty years of his life"

"With open arms I welcome 50... I’m so blessed to be here!"

- Pamela Anderson
"I have enjoyed greatly the second blooming… suddenly you find – at the age of 50, say – that a whole new life has opened before you"
- Agatha Christie
"By the time we hit 50, we have learned our hardest lessons. We have found out that only a few things are really important. We have learned to take life seriously, but never ourselves."
- Marie Dressler
"We don't stop playing because we grow old; we grow old because we stop playing."
- George Bernard Shaw
"I always knew that good stuff would come along when I was older. So when I was 18, I longed to be 30; when I was 30, I longed to be 50. I've always looked forward to my next birthday"

- Frans Kafka
